From Taiwan to the US

A prime example of spreading Asian Culture

History of Immigration

During the 90s, a large number of Taiwanese immigrants moved across the Atlantic to the United States, where most settled on the East and West Coasts and made homes for themselves there.

History Continued

Due to insufficient jobs, many of the people that immigrated West were young adults trying to find jobs and continue their education.
